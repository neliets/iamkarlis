Fourth semester

Pre-1930 residential housing renovation

Main goal of the semester is to learn about renovation. Mainly for buildings built before 1930's, the main points were that back fire escape had to be removed, apartments had to be changed and the building surveyed in person. After renovation concept, we specialized in each area to provide drawings - my was steel extension structure.

Semester included a large amount of non-project lectures, electives and activities. Such as two week study trip to Vietnam and collaboration with them, detailed sustainability lectures and electives(such as Life cycle costs, DGNB matrix etc), as well as leadership elective.
