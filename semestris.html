First semester

Single family house; introduction

Main goal of the semester is to introduce everybody to the construction industry and provide basic knowledge of the main concepts that construction industry is based on.

Practically, asside from lectures we were given a semester project to execute in groups. The majority of project work had to be executed by hand - hand-drawn. That is to provide more thought and insight in the small details of the construction. Revit was introduced in later stages to help prepare us for the second semester.
