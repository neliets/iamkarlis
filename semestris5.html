Fifth semester

Multi-storey building complex

Main goal of the semester is to learn about multi-storey buildings and design. This semester we were let to design our own building and encouraged to make it less conventional, so that we have a lot of problems to solve and challanges.

Challanges in load-bearing, planning, budget and building services(HVAC) are being resolved while keeping in mind client's list of wishes and demands.
Semester also includes a 6 week elective about BIM.
